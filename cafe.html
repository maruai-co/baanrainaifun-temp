Baanrainaifun Café & Restaurant

What began as a small café serving healthy drinks, coffee, and homemade baked goods has grown into a restaurant. Our restaurant offers Thai-style vegan and vegetarian home cooking, prepared with care and a love for simple, wholesome food. We place great importance on the ingredients we use and believe in being honest with our guests about where they come from. Some seasonal produce and plants are organically grown right here at Baan Rai Nai Fun. For ingredients that are not organic, we handle them with extra care — thoroughly rinsing and washing before preparing meal. Every dish reflects our philosophy: fresh, mindful, and nourishing food, made with sincerity.
